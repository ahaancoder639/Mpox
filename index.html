What is Mpox?

Mpox, formerly known as monkeypox, is a viral disease that can spread from animals to humans and between people. It is caused by the mpox virus, a member of the Orthopoxvirus genus.

Symptoms of Mpox

  • Fever
  • Rash and skin lesions
  • Swollen lymph nodes
  • Muscle aches
  • Chills and exhaustion

How to Prevent Mpox?

To reduce the risk of Mpox infection, follow these steps:

  • Practice good hygiene, such as frequent handwashing.
  • Avoid close contact with infected individuals.
  • Wear protective gear when caring for patients.
  • Avoid consuming undercooked meat from wild animals.
